About us

Founded in 1824, The Bath Royal Literary and Scientific Institution (also known as BRLSI) , is an independant educational charity exhibition based in Bath, England.
This website is dedicated into recreating the old Institution building.
Using the little resources that we were given, we have recreated it within a virtual environment.
These also includes the multiple artifacts that we've recreated with the help of photogrammetry. Enjoy the experience!

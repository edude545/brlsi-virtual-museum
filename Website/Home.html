About us

Founded in 1824, The Bath Royal Literary and Scientific Institution (also known as BRLSI) , is an independant educational charity exhibition based in Bath, England.
This is a virtual museum experience, where you can walk around a recreation of the old building and interact with artefacts.
We've digitised several of BRLSI's artefacts using photogrammetry software.
